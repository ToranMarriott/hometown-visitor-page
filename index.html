Visit Folkestone

Discover the charm of our coastal town

Things to See

Lower Leas Coastal Park

An 11-hectare cliff-side park with gardens, play-areas, wildlife zones, and an amphitheatre; a calm place with dramatic views.

Battle of Britain Memorial

A striking and moving monument to the pilots of WWII, overlooking the Channel — history & scenery in one.

East Cliff & Warren Country Park

Stunning coastal walks, cliffs and pretty beaches, plus wild nature and fossils if you feel exploratory.

Things to Do

Harbour Arm & Promenade

A vibrant stretch of food stalls, bars and cafés right out on the water with views and sea air — perfect for a sunset walk or casual drink.

Putters Crazy Golf on the Beach

A fun 12-hole mini-golf right next to the beach, combining seaside vibes with mild competition.

Enjoy Folkestone Creative Quarter

Galleries, independent shops, studios, street art, cafés — this is where the town’s art & culture feel organic and alive.

Getting Here

By Car

By Bus
